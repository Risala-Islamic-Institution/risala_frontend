## 🧠 **Core Psychological & Islamic Principles Used**

Before colors, the **thinking**:

* **Islamic psychology** values:
  → *Sakīnah (calm)*, *Thiqah (trust)*, *Hikmah (wisdom)*, *Nūr (light)*
* **Modern UX** demands:
  → clarity, low eye fatigue, emotional safety, premium feel
* **Avoided on purpose**:
  ❌ loud greens
  ❌ childish colors
  ❌ gold-overuse
  ❌ old “mosque poster” aesthetics

This is **Islamic × modern × global**, not cultural nostalgia.

# 🧩 **COLOR USAGE HIERARCHY (DO NOT BREAK THIS)**

1. **Primary (Emerald)** – identity & navigation
2. **Neutral Dark (Charcoal)** – text & structure
3. **Background (Pearl White)** – reading comfort
4. **Accent (Gold)** – meaning & focus
5. **Semantic colors** – clarity, not decoration

This hierarchy keeps the UI **calm and authoritative**.

---

# 🖋️ **LOGO COLOR STRATEGY**

**Primary logo version**
